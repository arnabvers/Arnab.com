The Journey

A documented evolution of logic, hardware, and security.

Phase 01: The Spark

Curiosity & Physics

It started with a deep fascination for the cosmos—black holes, relativity, and how systems govern reality. This theoretical curiosity laid the foundation for my deeply analytical mindset.

Phase 02: Tactile Logic

Hardware Engineering

Theory wasn't enough. I needed to build. Transitioning into microcontrollers like Arduino and motor drivers, I began bringing code into the physical world, constructing custom robotic vehicles.

Phase 03: The Network

Internet of Things (IoT)

I realized isolated hardware was limited. Integrating ESP8266 and ESP32 chips, I connected my physical builds to the cloud. I engineered smart room systems operating seamlessly via Alexa and Google APIs.

Phase 04: The Shield

Future Cybersecurity

As my environments became connected, I saw the vulnerabilities. My ultimate goal crystallized: mastering network protocols and encryption to secure the connected infrastructure of the future.
